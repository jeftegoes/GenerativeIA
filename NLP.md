# 1. Definition

- Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** focused on the interaction between computers and **human natural languages**.
  - In particular, how to program computers to process and analyze large amounts of natural language data.
- It aims to enable computers to **process, analyze, and understand large amounts of natural language data**.

# 2. Types of Data

- **Structured Data**
  - Has a predefined format.
  - **Examples:** Spreadsheets, tables, databases.
- **Unstructured Data**
  - Does not follow a predefined format.
  - **Examples:** Emails, chats, blogs, books, audio, video, and images.

# 4. Value of Unstructured Data

- **Enables**
  - Better understanding of customers.
  - Increased efficiency.
  - Business scalability.
- However, it is **harder to analyze and leverage** due to its lack of structure.

# 5. Why NLP Is Growing Rapidly

- NLP makes it possible to extract value from unstructured data.
- Companies are competing to be the first in their industries to **unlock insights** from this data.
- **Business Opportunities**
  - NLP opens opportunities to transform unstructured data into actionable insights.
  - Future tutorials focus on identifying how businesses can leverage NLP effectively.
